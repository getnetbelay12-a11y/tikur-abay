# Tikur Abay UAT Checklists

For each role, capture:
- page clarity
- missing info
- duplicate info
- broken action
- confusing workflow
- time to complete task

## Executive
- Can you answer the five executive questions in under 10 seconds?
- Are any cards or tables repeating the same metric?
- Did every executive drill-down open the expected page?

## Operations Manager
- Can you identify assignable, blocked, and due-soon vehicles quickly?
- Did the fleet board filters and drawers behave correctly?
- Were any statuses or locations unclear?

## Dispatcher
- Can you move from trip list to live map to driver report without losing context?
- Did trip and report actions behave as expected?
- Did chat context help or distract from dispatch work?

## Marketing
- Is the customer, lead, quote, and agreement flow easy to follow?
- Is any information missing when deciding next follow-up?
- Can you tell what inventory is available by branch?

## Finance
- Can you find open receivables and collections actions quickly?
- Is payment context clear from the drawer?
- Are route profitability and payout summaries understandable?

## HR
- Are onboarding, KYC, expiry alerts, and reviews due clearly separated?
- Is any people data missing from HR actions?
- Are follow-up actions obvious?

## Driver
- Are the mobile trip and report flows clear and fast?
- Can you upload documents and receive alerts without confusion?
- Are any labels mixed-language or unclear?

## Customer
- Can you find current trips, documents, invoices, and payments easily?
- Are statuses and next actions clear?
- Does any page feel empty or unfinished?
