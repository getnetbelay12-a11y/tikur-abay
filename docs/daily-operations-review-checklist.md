# Tikur Abay Daily Operations Review Checklist

## Daily Review Items
- Delayed trips
- Blocked vehicles
- Overdue maintenance
- Failed payments
- Unsigned agreements
- Failed uploads
- KYC issues
- User complaints

## Review Format

| Area | What to Check | Owner | Status | Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Delayed trips | Trips delayed beyond expected SLA | Operations | Open / Closed | Reassign, escalate, or update customer |
| Blocked vehicles | Units blocked from dispatch | Operations / Maintenance | Open / Closed | Resolve block reason |
| Overdue maintenance | Vehicles past due threshold | Maintenance | Open / Closed | Schedule service |
| Failed payments | Failed or pending payment flow records | Finance | Open / Closed | Retry or follow up |
| Unsigned agreements | Agreements stuck before signature | Marketing / Commercial | Open / Closed | Send follow-up |
| Failed uploads | Missing POD, receipts, or document uploads | Operations / Support | Open / Closed | Re-upload and verify |
| KYC issues | Driver KYC pending, rejected, or suspended | HR | Open / Closed | Review and update |
| User complaints | Customer or staff complaints | Support / Business owner | Open / Closed | Assign owner and ETA |

## End-of-Day Actions
- Record unresolved issues.
- Assign next owner and target resolution date.
- Escalate anything blocking trips, payments, agreements, or user access.
