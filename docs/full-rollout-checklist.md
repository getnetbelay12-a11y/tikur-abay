- Final permissions verified for every role

- Monitoring active
- Backups active
- Error tracking active
- Escalation contacts documented

## Final Verification
- Executive dashboard verified
- Operations Hub verified
- Trips, vehicles, maintenance, finance, agreements, documents, and notifications verified
- Mobile upload, signing, and payment flows verified
- Final go/no-go meeting completed
