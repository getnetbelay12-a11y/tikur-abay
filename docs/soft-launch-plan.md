# Tikur Abay Soft Launch Plan

## Scope
- Start with limited real users only.
- Enable a limited set of branches first.
- Limit active drivers and customers during the first wave.
- Keep daily issue review and rollback readiness active.

## Suggested Initial Rollout

### Users
- 1 executive sponsor
- 1 operations manager
- 1 dispatcher
- 1 finance owner
- 1 HR owner
- 1 marketing owner

### Branches
- Addis Ababa HQ
- One secondary branch with manageable fleet volume

### Operational Participants
- Small initial driver cohort
- Small initial customer cohort
- Limited agreement and payment volume during week 1

## Soft Launch Controls
- Use a daily issue log with severity, owner, workaround, and ETA.
- Run a daily review meeting for launch issues and production blockers.
- Freeze non-critical feature changes during the soft launch window.
- Keep demo and training material available for support staff.

## Daily Review Checklist
- Confirm critical dashboards load.
- Confirm trips, vehicles, maintenance, payments, and agreements are functioning.
- Review incidents, complaints, and failed flows.
- Review error logs and monitoring alerts.
- Confirm next-day fixes and owner assignments.
